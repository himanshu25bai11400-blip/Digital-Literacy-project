Discription-/Summary-

This project report documents my work as a Student Digital Ambassador.
Across five tasks, I explored key aspects of digital literacy - from creating an
awareness infographic, setting up a professional digital portfolio, exploring coding
and collaboration platforms, understanding proper email etiquette, and learning
about cybercrime and its prevention.
Each task built on the last, developing my ability to communicate, collaborate, and
stay safe in digital environments.

For Task 1, I was asked to create a one-page visual resource to help batchmates
understand what Digital Literacy means and why it matters

Task 2 involved setting up a digital portfolio to showcase my work, skills, and
achievements as a Student Digital Ambassador.

For Task 3, I explored a range of digital platforms used in education and professional
contexts, documenting my activity and reflecting on their purpose and features.

Task 4 required me to demonstrate understanding of professional email
communication by drafting sample emails and compiling an etiquette checklist.

Task 5 asked me to investigate a real-world cybercrime case study and produce a
write-up alongside a personal prevention checklist.


This project provided me with valuable knowledge and practical experience in digital
literacy and its real-world applications. Through the different tasks, I learned how to
use digital tools effectively, maintain a professional online presence, and
communicate responsibly in the digital environment. I also gained awareness about
cyber safety, online fraud, and the importance of protecting personal information.
